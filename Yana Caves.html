About Yana

Yana, one of the wettest villages in the world, is renowned for its pristine beauty and is the cleanest village in Karnataka,
earning the title of the second cleanest village in India.Located in the lush forests of the Katgal Range, Yana boasts
two unique rock outcrops, the Bhairaveshwara Shikhara and the Mohini Shikhara, easily accessible by trekking through the thick forests.

These massive rock formations, composed of solid black crystalline karst limestone, stand prominently in the Sahyadri hill range of
the Western Ghats. The Bhairaveshwara Shikhara, reaching a height of 120 meters, and the Mohini Shikhara, at 90 meters, are iconic
Yana is also a pilgrimage center, featuring a cave temple below the Bhairaveshwara Shikhara, housing a Swayambhu Linga with water
dripping from the roof, adding sanctity to the site.

Experience the magic of Yana during Shivaratri when a vibrant car festival takes place amidst other festivities.
The surrounding hillocks are known for their evergreen scenic forests, providing a tranquil escape for nature lovers.
